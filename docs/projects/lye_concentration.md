### Page 2

$$

Let the following variables have the values:

### Page 3

$$
M_{H_2O} = 2 * 1.008 + 16
$$

$$
M_{H_2O} = 18.016\space g \cdot mol^{-1}
$$

$$
V = m_L * \frac{(1-\frac{P_L}{100})M_w}{(\frac{P_L}{100})P_wM_L}
$$

where $L$ is for $NaOH$ and $w$ is for $H_2O$ (water).
